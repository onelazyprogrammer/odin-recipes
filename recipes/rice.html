Depressing Rice!

Description

So, I actually kinda love rice, I would like to, someday, make some fine ass chinese rice.

Ingredients
  • A cup of water
  • Half a cup of rice
  • A slice of cheese
  • A grain of salt
  • Some milk
Steps
  1. Put oil on the pot
  2. Drop the rice
  3. Wait until it is about to burn
  4. Drop the water
  5. Wait until it starts to boil
  6. Drop the salt, cheese and milk
